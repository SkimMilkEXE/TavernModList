> A Terraria mod pack held together by Discord chaos, bad ideas, and duct tape.

## What is this?

No grand theme, no roadmap — just whatever content sounds fun to add next. Somebody says something dumb or cool in the Discord, and a few days later it's a weapon, a potion, or a guinea pig with a god complex.

## What's Inside

### Potions & Chaos
- **Prime's Potion** — *(1% Moon Lord drop)* Slams literally every beneficial buff in the game onto you at once, tucked behind a single icon so your buff bar doesn't implode.
- **Heavenly Brew** — *(Empress of Light drop, also craftable)* Heals you, makes you briefly untouchable, then leaves you faster and tankier for a few minutes. A gift from beyond the clouds, apparently.
- **Golden Fruit** — A shimmering fruit trees occasionally drop. Definitely not an infinite money glitch. 👀

### Gear & Gadgets
- **Iron Suit** — A full Iron Man-style power armor set for the post-Moon Lord crowd. Flight, a blaster, and a unibeam included.
- **Prime Specs** — Mech-boss-soul-powered glasses that read out an enemy's HP and defense the moment you hit them.
- **Remote Detonator** — Fires rockets. Nobody has explained why. Nobody wants to.
- **Guinea Pig Staff** — Summons a guinea pig that farts rainbows and shoots them at your enemies.
- **Lucky Clover** — Boosts your fishing luck for the anglers among us.

### Quality of Life
- **Item Browser** — A searchable, rebindable-hotkey UI listing every item this mod adds, because scrolling through a wiki is for suckers.

## Building

This is a standard [tModLoader](https://github.com/tModLoader/tModLoader) mod. Open it from tModLoader's Mod Sources menu (or via the `.csproj`) and build like any other mod project.
